About Craft-E

We are a social crafting platform that connects aspiring crafting learners to crafting professionals by offering a range of crafting workshops provided by our crafting teachers. We make booking, scheduling and payment of workshops easy by us handling the admin processes. We lift the burden of the admin duties off our crafting teachers so they can focus on mastering their crafts.

Popular Crafting Categories

Sewing

We offer a range of sewing workshops from begginer level to advanced level. We work closely with our sewing professionals to make the best sewing workshops available at an affordable price.



Knitting

Knitting is one of the most popular forms of crafts and has enjoyed a massive ressurgence. We are proud to offer knitting workshops on our platform. In addition to providing workshops through our platform we will be building a community of aspiring knitting learners and knitting professionals.

Jewellery Making

Making jewellery is extremely satisfying, as anyone who has experience of this will tell you!. We will be providing a range of jewellery making workshop experiences to our learners. Soon you will be creating your own necklace or bracelet and will be proudly wearing your own creation.
